What Is Lightspeed Amazon Integration?

Lightspeed Amazon integration is the process of connecting your Lightspeed Retail or Lightspeed eCom system with Amazon Seller Central. Once integrated, product listings, inventory levels, orders, and shipment updates can be synchronized automatically between the two platforms. Instead of managing data manually in multiple systems, retailers can operate everything from Lightspeed while selling actively on Amazon.

With SKUPlugs acting as the integration bridge, retailers gain real-time data flow and complete control over their multichannel operations.
